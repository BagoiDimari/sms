The School Establishment

Babs International Academy was established in 2009 to provide quality school education for the country to train intelligent future leaders.

At the time the school was established, only the nursery and primary school were functioning. As the school was growing over the years the need to provide space for secondary school arose therefore the school tried its possible best to build the secondary school session and Alhamdulillah it was done successfully.

Babs International School got accredited by W.A.E.C and N.E.C.O in the year 2010 and students have written thier first external exams successfully without any delay and complaints from the various centers. Babs International Academy has over 1200 students as at the time the school was commissioned but currently the school has about 2500 students studying.

Babs International Academy is located at NO:110022 karkara street, jabba, Abuja.
