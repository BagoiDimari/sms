How The SMS Works


Teachers

The SMS website reduces the amount of physical work teachers have to go through such as student record keeping, student assesment result record and much more etc...

Parents

The SMS website allows parents to directly communicate with teachers, it also allows parents to directly go to their childs dashboard with the use of the childs surname.

Students

The SMS website allows students to view their grades after being uploaded by their respective teachers, also it allows students to access thier class materials and lots more.
